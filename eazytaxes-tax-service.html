Common Questions
Everything you want to know
Clear, honest answers to the questions we hear most.
How long does the whole process take?
Most clients complete their profiling in about 5 minutes and the detailed questionnaire in 15-30 minutes. After that, your expert typically reviews and prepares your return within 1-3 business days. Average total turnaround is 3-5 days.
What if I don't have all my documents ready?
No problem. Your dashboard saves progress automatically, so you can start the questionnaire, upload what you have, and come back anytime. Your expert won't begin the final review until you've marked it complete.
Can I talk to my tax expert directly?
Yes. Your dashboard includes secure messaging that connects you directly with your assigned CPA or Enrolled Agent. They typically respond within a few hours during business days. No phone tag, no waiting on hold.
When do I pay, and how is my price determined?
After your free 5-minute profile, we'll confirm your exact price based on the complexity of your tax situation. No payment is required until your return has been fully prepared and you've had the chance to review and approve it.
What types of tax returns do you handle?
We handle a wide range of individual tax situations including W-2 employment, freelance and self-employment income (1099s), rental income, investment gains and losses, stock options, multi-state filings, and major life events like marriage, home purchases, or having a child.
What happens after my return is filed?
Your dashboard continues as your record. You'll get confirmation the moment the IRS accepts your return. Download copies anytime. And if the IRS ever reaches out, your expert is available to help - your relationship doesn't end when you file.
